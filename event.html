Nigerian tertiary institutions (nationwide)

About the competition

The Augustine Udefa Speaking Scholarship showcased students' critical thinking and public speaking skills. Participants presented recorded or live speeches and were judged on clarity, substance and impact. Three finalists were selected — the winner received a full scholarship and a cash prize; runners-up received cash awards.

Public Speaking Competition Scholarship

Prizes

  • Winner: Full scholarship + cash prize (N50,000)
  • 1st Runner-up: Cash prize (N100,000)
  • 2nd Runner-up: Cash prize (N50,000)

Pre-establishment Events

As one interested in humanitarian service, Augustine Udefa, the founder of the Augustine Udefa Foundation, has been in the spotlight contributing to education and healthy living.

2017 Lafia, Nasarawa State

Donation of 400 Mosquito Nets

In 2017, Augustine carried out selfless service to a community in Nigeria during his national youth service. He donated four hundred long-lasting insecticidal mosquito nets to boarding student in Government College, Lafia, Nasarawa State, Nigeria. This was in a bid to help the students prevent malaria infection as malaria was prevalent in the community at the time

Health Education Community
2017 Lafia, Nasarawa State

Donation of 20 School Desks

In addition, Augustine donated twenty desks to Government Secondary School, Ombi 1, Lafia, another school in the community. Augustine’s donation to the school was timely as some of the classrooms had insufficient desks for the students to sit and learn comfortably.

Education Infrastructure
2017 Lafia, Nasarawa State

Donation of five suggestions/complaint boxes

Augustine also donated five suggestions/complaints boxes to Dalhatu Araf Specialist Hospital, Lafia, also in Nasarawa State.

Academic Excellence & Awards

Augustine is a lover of education and finds it fulfilling when students excel academically. In 2020, he did part-time teaching in St. George Sure Foundation Academy, a secondary school located in Calabar, Nigeria, where he taught the students mathematics, physics, and chemistry.

He initiated the organization of award ceremonies and presented academic prizes to the best students in the subjects he taught, inspiring excellence and motivating students to reach their full potential.
