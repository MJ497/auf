Augustine Udefa Speaking Scholarship — Maiden Edition (2025)

The Augustine Udefa Foundation is pleased to announce that the maiden edition of our speaking scholarship competition was a huge success. (Competition: April 6, 2025 — October 18, 2025. Award ceremony: October 29, 2025.)

Our Foundation is pleased to announce that the maiden edition of our speaking scholarship competition was a huge success. The competition started on April 6, 2025 and ended on October 18, 2025. The 2025 award ceremony took place on October 29, 2025 in the College Hall of the College of Medical Sciences at the University of Calabar, Nigeria.

We wish to use this medium to thank key players who contributed to the success of the competition. Our profound gratitude goes to Almighty God for the grace and blessings to champion this cause. We are grateful to the Founder, Augustine Udefa, and Co-Founder, Precious Udefa, for the vision to establish this Foundation.

The Augustine Udefa Speaking Scholarship competition 2025 would not have been successful without the support and selfless service of our board and team members. We are very grateful to Sunday Okpa, Board Secretary; Efiok Archibong, PhD, Programs Director; Charles Udefa, Board Member; Charles-Vic Anyati, Graphic Designer and Director of Publicity; and Esther Orindu, Programs Review Writer. These guys have been very instrumental to the success of the 2025 competition and the smooth running of the Foundation’s activities. God bless you all. We also thank our technical team for ensuring that the technical department runs efficiently.

We are sincerely grateful to our judges for the significant role they played in the success of the event. Their feedback and constructive criticism, as well as their sincere, transparent, and professional judgement were topnotch.

Our gratitude goes to the Provost and Deputy Provost of the College of Medical Sciences, as well as the Dean of the Faculty of Basic Medical Sciences of the University of Calabar for giving us the opportunity to host our maiden scholarship award ceremony in the College and for gracing the event. We also thank the Ag Head as well as the Chief Technologist of the Department of Physiology, the Deputy Director of Students Support Center, and other academics and non-academics at the University of Calabar who attended and graced the event.

Our gratitude extends to our families, friends, and followers who have been very supportive. Thank you for your support.

Scholarship Awards

  1. Ibiangake Ezekiel Akpan (Winner): Augustine Udefa Speaking Scholarship + ₦50,000.
  2. Deborah Effiong Ephraim (1st Runner-up): One-time scholarship of ₦100,000.
  3. Nkoyo Asuquo Nyong (2nd Runner-up): One-time scholarship of ₦50,000.

Other participants received consolation prizes.
