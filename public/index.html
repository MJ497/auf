About the Foundation

Our Mission

The Augustine Udefa Foundation is committed to improving the lives of people, especially those with the fewest resources, by providing opportunities for them to access free education and quality health services.

Our Vision

The Foundation envisages a future where free educational institutions are established, and health services provided free of charge to the less privileged and financially disadvantaged people in our society. We believe that every life is important, and education is the bedrock of a nation. So, everyone, including the poor, should have the privilege to access health care and the formal education they desire and need.

Augustine Udefa

Founder & Chairman

Augustine Udefa is a teacher, researcher, and philanthropist. He began his elementary education at Command Children’s School, Calabar; started his secondary education at Stella Maris Seminary, Uyanga, and completed it at St. Patrick’s College, Calabar, all in Nigeria.

Augustine attended the University of Calabar, Nigeria, where he obtained a bachelor’s degree in physiology, a master’s degree in physiology, and a post graduate diploma in education. He is currently pursuing a doctoral degree in nutrition and exercise physiology at the University of Missouri-Columbia.

Augustine is a charismatic individual with a love for humanitarian service. He founded the Augustine Udefa Foundation because of his love for humanity. Augustine believes that the world would be a better place if every privileged person gave a helping hand to one or two people in need.

His burning desire for service to humanity led to the establishment of the Foundation because he believes that, through this Foundation, many people, especially those with the fewest resources, will have access to free education and quality health services.

Scholarship Programs

We offer comprehensive support to students at all academic levels.

Augustine Udefa Speaking Scholarship

The Augustine Udefa Speaking Scholarship is awarded to students in African educational institutions, who can demonstrate good public speaking skills and critical thinking ability.

  • Full school fee support for the period of your program.
Apply now

Augustine Udefa Orphan Scholarship

The Augustine Udefa Orphan Scholarship is awarded to people who have lost both parents and do not have any sponsor to support their education

  • For Secondary & university students
  • minimum cumulative grade point average (CGPA) of 3.5 on a 5.0 scale or 2.5 on a 4.0 scale
Apply now

Augustine Udefa Excellence Scholarship

The Augustine Udefa Excellence Scholarship is a highly competitive scholarship awarded to students with excellent academic profile.

  • Minimum CGPA of 4.30 out of 5.0 or 3.30 out of 4.0
  • Full school fees coverage + stipend
Apply now

AUSMP — Augustine Udefa Scholarship Mentorship Program

Mentorship & full-scholarship preparation for excellent but underfunded applicants from Africa seeking graduate study in the United States. .

  • Application deadline: 31 March.
  • Mentorship & preparation: 15 April – 30 October.
  • Scholarship start: Fall or Spring semester (depending on award).
